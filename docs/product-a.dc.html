About the film

A man wanders out of the desert with no memory of the years he's lost, and slowly walks back toward the family he left behind. Wim Wenders' aching American road movie — all neon, big skies, and Ry Cooder's slide guitar — is one of the great films about distance, between places and between people. This is the Criterion 4K restoration, supervised by Wenders himself.
